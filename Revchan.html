About the creator

My name is Ken Takahashi, I am a student and entrepreneur from Guetersloh, Germany. During my application process, I struggled with finding affordable schools. It took me quite a while but I ultimately came up with a list of budget friendly schools. And after stumbling across the IntltoUSA subreddit, I got engaged in helping prospective international students with the same problem I had. Fast forward a few months and I decided to create a website to find match schools based on ones profile and that's how Revchan (a word derived from the phrase Reverse Chance Me) came into life.
